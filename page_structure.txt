     <h2 class="h2-title">
      go back on one's word 食言，反悔，违背诺言
     </h2>

     <p style="white-space: pre-line">
      go back on one's word

汉语意思：

食言，违背诺言
反悔，不守信用
说话不算数

例句：

A true gentleman never goes back on his word.
真正的绅士从不食言。

The contractor went back on his word and demanded more money.
承包商违背了诺言，要求更多钱。
     </p>
